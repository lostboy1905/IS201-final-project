Crypto projects worth looking into:

  1. Bitcoin
    • First crypto currency
    • Largest by market cap
  2. Ethereum
    • Second largest by market cap
    • By far the most used for smart contracts
  3. Solana
    • Known as "the Ethereum killer" because of its use cases
    • Allegedly easier to work with than Ethereum
    • One of the cheapest blockchains to use
  4. Litecoin
    • Meant to be used as a daily alternative to Bitcoin
    • Faster transaction speed than Bitcoin
